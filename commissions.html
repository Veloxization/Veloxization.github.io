TOS

Regarding payment

I take payments through PayPal in EUR (€) only. Use the URL paypal.me/velopaypal to send payments.

Additional fees may be added if your request is especially complicated. I will always negotiate with you about additional fees if I feel like your piece will take longer than usual.

Regarding refunds

If you change your mind before I start drawing or you're not happy with the sketch, feel free to ask for a refund.

Regarding WIPs

To prevent art theft, I won't send WIPs before the payment has been processed and all WIPs will be watermarked and screenshots instead of full resolution image files.

By default, I will send a WIP after each stage but you can request as many as you want even mid-stage.

Please request any major changes to the line art before I start colouring. It's very difficult to edit anything major when the colour is already in.

Regarding usage

An art piece you bought can naturally be used in any way you like. I would, of course, appreciate credit if someone asks.

What I will draw?

In general, humans and anthros. Feel free to ask if I am able to do your specific request.

What I won't draw?

I won't take characters that don't have a reference sheet or an otherwise clear image I can use as a reference.
